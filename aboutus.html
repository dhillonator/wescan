Frequently Asked Questions

How long does artificial turf last?

With proper maintenance, our premium artificial turf can last 15-20 years. The actual lifespan depends on usage and environmental factors, but our installations are designed for long-term durability.

Is artificial turf safe for children and pets?

Yes, our artificial turf is completely safe for children and pets. We use non-toxic materials that are lead-free and meet strict safety standards. The turf is also designed with proper drainage to maintain a clean and hygienic surface.

What maintenance is required?

Artificial turf requires minimal maintenance compared to natural grass. Regular brushing to maintain the turf's upright position, occasional rinsing to remove dust, and prompt cleaning of any spills or pet waste is typically all that's needed.

How does artificial turf handle BC's weather?

Our artificial turf is specifically chosen for British Columbia's climate. It's designed to handle heavy rainfall with excellent drainage systems, and it's UV-stabilized to prevent fading from sun exposure. The turf also performs well in cold temperatures and light snow.

What is the installation process like?

Installation typically takes 2-4 days depending on the size of your yard. The process includes ground preparation, base installation, turf laying, and finishing touches. We handle everything from start to finish, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily routine.
